Calico Cats

Genetics Simulator

Calicos are cats with a mix of black, red (or orange), and white spots. Many cultures believe they bring good fortune, and have granted them nicknames such as "lucky cats" and "money cats".

Whether or not they actually bring good luck is up for debate, but calicos have another peculiarity that sets them apart: They are, except in rare genetic circumstances, always female.

Calico Genetics

The genes that determine a cat's fur color exist only on the X chromosome. Since female cats have two X chromosomes, they can have two colors at the same time.
